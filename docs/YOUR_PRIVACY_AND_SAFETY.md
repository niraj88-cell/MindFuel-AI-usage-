# Your Privacy & Safety on SatyaShift

SatyaShift helps you understand where your attention really goes — quietly, in the
background — so you can focus better. Because that means looking at your browsing, we
designed it from day one to protect you. Here's exactly how, in plain language.

---

## We see *domains*, never your content

SatyaShift records **which website you were on and for how long** — for example,
`youtube.com` for 12 minutes. That's it.

We **do not** record:
- the specific video, post, or page you viewed
- the page's text, your searches, or anything you type
- your passwords, messages, or form data

So we can tell you "you spent 40 minutes on short-form video yesterday" — but never
*what* you watched.

## Some places are never tracked — at all

These are skipped automatically, before anything is ever recorded:
- **Banking & payments** (your bank, PayPal, Venmo, etc.)
- **Health** (doctors, patient portals, medical sites)
- **Email** (Gmail, Outlook)
- **Password managers** (1Password, LastPass, Bitwarden)
- **Anything in Incognito / private windows**

## Your data is *yours* — and only yours

Your browsing data is locked to your account at the database level. **No other user —
not even people in your support squad — can ever see which sites you visit.** When you
join a squad, your teammates can only see your **name, photo, and whether you completed
a focus session** — never your browsing history.

## No blocking. No shame. No leaderboards.

SatyaShift doesn't lock you out of sites or rank you against others. There are no scores
to compete on — just gentle, private awareness and supportive check-ins. The goal is a
calmer relationship with your attention, not guilt.

## Your focus sessions are honest

When SatyaShift says you had a "deep focus" session, that's measured from real activity —
it can't be faked or inflated. And if we don't have enough information to judge a
session, we say so honestly ("unverified") rather than pretending. We'd rather be
truthful than flattering.

## Your account and connection are protected

- You sign in securely; the app talks to its servers only over encrypted (HTTPS)
  connections.
- Automatic limits prevent abuse and keep the service stable for everyone.

## You're always in control

- **Export your data** whenever you want.
- **Delete your account and all your data** at any time — it's permanent and complete.

---

**The short version:** SatyaShift sees the *shape* of your attention, never the private
details. Your data stays yours, you're never blocked or shamed, and you can take it all
with you — or erase it — whenever you choose.
